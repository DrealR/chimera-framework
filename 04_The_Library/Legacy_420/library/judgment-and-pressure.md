# JUDGMENT AND PRESSURE — THE DEEP CONNECTIONS

## TYPE 1 vs TYPE 2 NAMING — THE TIME DIMENSION

```
TYPE 1 NAMING (Present/Descriptive):
├── "This is a lion"
├── What is it RIGHT NOW?
├── No story needed
├── No comparison needed
├── No past or future required
├── Just THIS, HERE, NOW
└── PRESENT MOMENT OBSERVATION

TYPE 2 NAMING (Judgment/Time-based):
├── "This is EVIL"
├── Requires comparison to a STANDARD
├── Standard comes from PAST (what should be)
├── Or FUTURE (what this will cause)
├── "Evil" needs a story, a context, a timeline
├── Can't be evil without reference to something else
└── LEAVES THE PRESENT MOMENT
```

## JUDGMENT REQUIRES LEAVING THE PRESENT

```
TO JUDGE SOMETHING, YOU NEED:

1. COMPARISON (to what it "should" be)
   └── The "should" comes from PAST (memory of standard)

2. CONSEQUENCE (what it will cause)
   └── The "will cause" comes from FUTURE (projection)

3. CATEGORY (good/evil, right/wrong)
   └── Categories come from ACCUMULATED PAST experience

YOU CANNOT JUDGE IN PURE PRESENT.

In pure present, there is only WHAT IS.
"The lion is here."
Not "the lion is dangerous" (future projection).
Not "the lion shouldn't be here" (past standard).
Just: "The lion is here."

JUDGMENT = TIME TRAVEL
You leave NOW to compare to THEN or LATER.
```

## HOW JUDGMENT CREATES PRESSURE

```
THE MECHANISM:

1. JUDGMENT CREATES A "SHOULD"
   ├── "I should be better"
   ├── "This should be different"
   ├── "They should have done X"
   └── A standard is created

2. "SHOULD" vs "IS" = GAP
   ├── Reality is one way
   ├── Judgment says it should be another
   ├── The gap between them creates tension
   └── Tension = PRESSURE

3. MORE JUDGMENTS = MORE GAPS = MORE PRESSURE
   ├── Judge yourself → pressure on self
   ├── Judge others → pressure on relationships
   ├── Judge the world → pressure everywhere
   └── Everything becomes HEAVY

4. PRESSURE SEEKS TO COLLAPSE
   ├── Like deep sea on submarine
   ├── The pressure wants IN
   ├── Wants to CRUSH the difference
   ├── Wants to make inside = outside
   └── Collapse = becoming the judgment

REMOVE JUDGMENT → NO "SHOULD" → NO GAP → NO PRESSURE → NO COLLAPSE
```
