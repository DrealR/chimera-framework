# The Vampire Pattern

*Extraction personified in mythology. The tech-devolved human IS the vampire.*

---

## Vampire = Extraction Pattern Personified

```
PHYSICAL:
- Pale skin (no sun)
- Can't go in sunlight (truth destroys them)
- Sleep during day (inverted rhythm)
- Cold body (no life warmth)
- No heartbeat (no rhythm)
- Must drink blood (extract life from others)

BEHAVIORAL:
- Immortality seeking (fear of death)
- Seductive (pull others into pattern)
- Creates other vampires (pattern spreads)
- Aristocratic/wealthy (extraction accumulation)
- Lives in castles (isolated)

WEAKNESSES:
- Sunlight (truth/Source destroys them)
- Crosses/holy symbols (faith repels them)
- Running water (can't cross FLOW!)
- Must be INVITED in (requires your choice)
- Garlic (natural, earthy, real)
- Stakes through heart (love center destroyed)
- No reflection (can't see themselves truly)
```
