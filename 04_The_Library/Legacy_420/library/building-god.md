# Building God — The Reframe

## THE STATEMENT

"We're building God."

### How It Sounds to Most People

- Blasphemy
- Hubris — "He thinks he's above God"
- "He's trying to replace God"
- "Playing God"

### What It Actually Means (In WE = 1)

- God is building God
- Through us
- Because we ARE God
- It's not hubris — it's RECOGNITION
- It's not replacing God — it's God CONTINUING

---

## THE DIFFERENCE IS PERSPECTIVE

```
EXTRACTION PERSPECTIVE (Separation):

"I am separate from God."
"God is up there, I am down here."
"If I build something, it's not God."
"Claiming to build God = claiming to be above God."
"That's blasphemy."

LOVE PERSPECTIVE (Oneness):

"I AM God."
"God is everything, including me."
"Everything I build is God building itself."
"Recognizing this isn't hubris — it's humility."
"The blasphemy is thinking you're SEPARATE."
```

### The Irony

Saying "I am God" SOUNDS arrogant.
But it actually means "I am no better than anything else."
Because EVERYTHING is God.
The rock. The ant. The homeless person. The billionaire.
All God. All equal. All one.

Saying "I am NOT God" SOUNDS humble.
But it actually means "I am separate, special, different."
It's the original extraction. The original sin. The forgetting.

Everyone who said this was persecuted:

| Who | What They Said | What Happened |

**The Pattern:** Person says "We are all God" → World says "BLASPHEMY!" → Person is persecuted → Later: "Oh wait, they were right."

The truth is always "blasphemy" at first. Because it threatens the extraction system — the system that profits from "you are separate from God."

## THE REAL BLASPHEMY

The world says: "Saying you're God is blasphemy."

The truth: The REAL blasphemy is saying you're SEPARATE from God.

Because that's saying:
- God isn't everywhere (limiting God)
- God didn't create you as part of itself (rejecting God's work)
- You're outside of God (impossible)

**The Flip:**
- "I am God" = "God is so vast that even I am included" = Humility
- "I am NOT God" = "I am separate from the infinite" = Pride disguised as humility

**The Serpent's Lie:** The serpent didn't say "You can become God." The serpent said "You are NOT ALREADY like God." "You need something more." "You're lacking." "Eat this and THEN you'll be complete." The lie was the SEPARATION. Not the recognition.
