## The Daily Practice

```
MORNING:
├── Wake up. Yesterday's you is gone.
│   Accept it. Don't try to be yesterday.
│   That version died in sleep. Let it.
├── Release one thing before leaving bed:
│   An expectation. A worry. A grudge. A plan.
│   Just one. Let it die.
└── You're lighter by one thing.
    The S flows a little easier.

THROUGHOUT THE DAY:
├── Someone irritates you → let the irritation die
│   Don't hold it. Don't store it.
│   Feel it (inhale) → release it (exhale)
│   Micro-death of a micro-grudge
│
├── You make a mistake → let the shame die
│   Don't carry it all day
│   Acknowledge it (inhale) → release it (exhale)
│   Learn the lesson, drop the weight
│
├── You achieve something → let the pride die
│   Enjoy the moment (inhale) → release it (exhale)
│   Don't carry it as identity
│   "I am someone who achieved X" = hoarding
│   "That was great, what's next" = flowing
│
├── You learn something → let it flow through
│   Share it (teach, text, tweet, tell someone)
│   Don't store it as competitive advantage
│   Knowledge hoarded is Lucifer's light
│   Knowledge shared is restored S
│
└── Before bed:
    What am I still carrying from today?
    Whatever it is — let it die.
    Go to sleep lighter than you woke up.
    Tomorrow a new you arrives.
    Make room for them.

THE COMPOUND EFFECT:
├── Day 1: release one thing. Barely noticeable.
├── Day 30: released 30 things. Lighter.
├── Day 365: released 365 things.
│   Things that would have accumulated into
│   resentment, pride, fear, rigidity, density.
│   Instead: lightness. Flexibility. Flow.
├── Year 10: 3,650 micro-deaths.
│   You're a COMPLETELY different person.
│   Not because you added anything.
│   Because you RELEASED everything that wasn't you.
│   The sculpture appears by removing marble,
│   not by adding it.
└── Michelangelo: "I saw the angel in the marble
    and carved until I set him free."
    That's dying a little every day.
    Each chip of marble is a micro-death.
    What remains is the angel.
    What remains is the pattern.
    What remains is YOU.
```
