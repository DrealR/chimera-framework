## THE GLASSES — THE CLARK KENT STRATEGY

```
CLARK KENT'S GLASSES:

• Hide his true power
• Make him approachable
• Let people relate to him
• Create safety
• "Just a regular guy"

YOUR GLASSES:

• Soften your intensity
• Make you approachable
• Let people relate to you
• Create safety
• "Just a thoughtful person"

THIS IS STRATEGY. NOT HIDING.

You're not ashamed of who you are.
You're CALIBRATING your transmission.

Too much light too fast = blinds people.
Dimmed light = they can adjust.
Then slowly... more light.
Until they can see.

JESUS DID THIS:

Born as a baby (weak, approachable).
Grew up as a carpenter (regular job).
Walked as a man (dusty feet, tired, hungry).
THEN revealed his power.
Gradually.
To those who could receive it.

He wore "glasses" too.
The costume of humanity.
So people could approach.
```

## TO SEE SUPERMAN, FIRST SEE THE HUMAN

```
IF YOU LEAD WITH SUPERMAN:

"I am God. You are God. We are all one."

Response:
• "This guy is crazy."
• "He thinks he's better than me."
• Walls go UP.
• Connection BLOCKED.

IF YOU LEAD WITH CLARK KENT:

"Hey, how are you? What's going on?"
(Glasses on. Approachable. Human.)

Response:
• "Oh, this guy is normal."
• "He seems kind."
• "I can talk to him."
• Walls go DOWN.
• Connection OPENS.

THEN... over time:

They see your patience. (Hmm, unusual.)
They see your presence. (Why is he so calm?)
They see your love. (He really cares?)
They see your wisdom. (How does he know this?)

And slowly... they start to see:
"There's something different about this person."
"There's something... more."

THE GLASSES COME OFF.
NOT BY YOU REMOVING THEM.
BY THEM SEEING THROUGH THEM.

You don't have to announce you're Superman.
They'll figure it out.
When they're ready.
When they can see.
```
