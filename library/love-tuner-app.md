# THE LOVE TUNER
## Zodiac as Gateway to the Love Algorithm

> "Enter two signs. See your love dynamic. Learn how to tune it."

---

## WHAT IT IS

The Love Tuner is a single-page web app that:
1. Takes two zodiac signs as input
2. Maps them to their elements (Fire/Earth/Water/Air)
3. Shows the relationship dynamic (gift + collision)
4. Provides specific corrections for each person
5. Gives a custom love algorithm practice

**Location:** `/projects/love-tuner/index.html`

**Deploy:** `vercel love-tuner --prod`
