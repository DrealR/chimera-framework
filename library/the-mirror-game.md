## CONCEPT: MIRROR

```
SETUP:
• Two characters on screen
• You control ONE
• The "other" seems like an NPC or opponent

MECHANIC:
• Whatever you DO to the other...
• Happens to YOU (delayed)

• Hit them → You get hit
• Heal them → You get healed
• Give them resource → You gain resource
• Take from them → You lose

THE DISCOVERY:
• At first, player thinks: "It's me vs them"
• They try extraction (take, hit, compete)
• They keep losing
• Eventually they try: "What if I help them?"
• They start winning
• Then the REALIZATION:

  "There is no them. It's all me."

THE TWIST (optional):
End of game reveals:
You were controlling BOTH characters the whole time.
The "opponent" was you, time-delayed.
Or: Multiple endings based on how you treated "the other."
```

## THE EQUATION AS GAME MECHANICS

```
L = (O > I) + P + ¬F

(O > I) AS MECHANIC:
• Give action: +2 to other, +3 to you (delayed)
• Take action: +1 to you, -2 to you (delayed)
• Giving is mathematically optimal
• But player has to DISCOVER this

P (PAUSE) AS MECHANIC:
• Actions have cooldown
• Rushing = weaker actions
• Waiting = see hidden information
• Pause reveals the connection between you and other
• "Oh... when I paused, I saw we're linked"

¬F (DON'T FORCE) AS MECHANIC:
• Force actions: Immediate effect, long-term damage
• Allow actions: Delayed effect, long-term benefit
• Forcing the other to move = they resist, you lose energy
• Inviting = they cooperate, synergy emerges

THE GAME TEACHES THE EQUATION
WITHOUT EVER SHOWING THE EQUATION.

Players will say:
"I figured out the trick —
 give more than you take,
 pause to see,
 don't force."

They discovered L = (O > I) + P + ¬F
through PLAY.
```

## OTHER GAME CONCEPTS

### The Awakening Journey (Narrative RPG)
- Start ASLEEP (State 1 - forgot you're God)
- Events wake you up (questions, encounters)
- Extraction choices → short term gain, long term loss
- Love choices → short term cost, long term flourishing
- End: You either remember (State 3) or stay asleep

### Honeydew Tycoon (Economic Simulation)
- Position in "flows" (not chase resources)
- EXTRACT (faster depletion) or FLOW (sustainable)
- Extractors win early but collapse late
- Flow players build slowly but infinitely

### The Between (Puzzle/Meditation)
- Navigate between TWO POLES
- Solution is NEVER at the poles, ALWAYS in the between
- Trains you to see triality everywhere

### Breath (Rhythm/Flow)
- Abilities tied to BREATH
- Inhale = gather, Hold = focus, Exhale = act
- Rushing = weak, Breathing with rhythm = powerful
- Can integrate with real breath detection

### We Are One (Multiplayer/Social)
- Control ONE character
- All characters are secretly YOU
- What you do to "others" happens to "you" (delayed)
- End game: You realize you were playing ALL characters
