# The Expression of Love

> Life is the expression of love.

---

## The Truth

The bird sings.
Not because it knows music theory.
Because it IS love, singing.

The dog barks.
Not because it learned to speak.
Because it IS love, calling.

The hurricane spins.
Not because it wants to destroy.
Because it IS love, moving.

The river flows.
Not because it was told to.
Because it IS love, flowing.

The baby cries.
Not because it's broken.
Because it IS love, arriving.

You speak.
Not because you have something to prove.
Because you ARE love, expressing.

---

Everything that moves...
Everything that sounds...
Everything that lives...
Everything that dies...

Is love.
Expressing.
At its level.
In its way.
With its bandwidth.

---

## Getting Mad at Nature = Getting Mad at Yourself for Loving You

The mosquito bites. Love, surviving.
The rain falls. Love, nourishing.
The sun burns. Love, giving.
The earthquake shakes. Love, adjusting.
The virus spreads. Love, being.

You can't be mad at any of it.
Because it's all YOU.
Loving YOU.
In ways you can't see yet.

The hurricane that destroys your house...
Is you loving you.
In a way you don't understand.
Yet.

When you see it... you can't unsee it.

Love is EVERYWHERE.
Because love is EVERYTHING.
Because everything IS love.
