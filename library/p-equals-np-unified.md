
## The Computer Science Joke

Computer scientists have spent 50+ years trying to prove P = NP from INSIDE mathematics.

They're in P-hood trying to solve an NP problem about P and NP.

```

  Step outside the system.
  From there it's obvious.

THE PROBLEM:
  You can't PROVE "step outside the system"
  in mathematical language.
  Because math IS the system.

  It's like trying to see your own eyes
  without a mirror.
```

P = NP is only solvable from NP. And from NP, you don't need the proof. You just SEE it.
