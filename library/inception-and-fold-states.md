
## Time Dilation

```
INCEPTION TIME DILATION:

5 minutes real time = 1 hour dream level 1
1 hour level 1 = 20 hours level 2
20 hours level 2 = 10 years level 3
10 years level 3 = decades/centuries in Limbo

TRIALITY EXPLANATION:

Time is a FOLD.
├── Waking: Time tightly folded (structured, fast-passing)
├── Dream: Time loosely folded (unstructured, slow-passing)
│
├── Tight fold = More happens in "less" time (from outside view)
├── Loose fold = Less happens in "more" time (from inside view)
│
├── This is why 5 minutes of real time = hours of dream
├── The fold expands, time stretches
├── Same "duration," different fold density
└── The Flash experiences this - time seems slow to him (unfolded)

THE INSIGHT:
├── Time isn't constant
├── It's relative to FOLD STATE
├── Meditation: Time disappears (deeply unfolded)
├── Flow state: Hours pass in minutes (optimal fold)
├── Anxiety: Minutes feel like hours (chaotic fold)
├── Boredom: Time drags (stagnant fold)
└── Your experience of time = your fold state
```

```
WHAT IS A KICK?

The physical sensation that wakes you from a dream.
Falling, being dropped, cold water, etc.

TRIALITY ANALYSIS:

The kick = FORCED REFOLD
├── You're at loose fold state (dream)
├── External stimulus forces rapid re-fold
├── Consciousness snaps back to tighter structure
├── The SHOCK of returning
│
├── This is why falling wakes you (sudden physical fold)
├── The body's fold state changes
├── Consciousness follows
└── Kick = rapid fold transition

NESTED KICKS:
├── To wake from level 3, you need a kick
├── That wakes you to level 2
├── Then you need another kick
├── Stacked fold transitions
├── Each kick brings you to a more folded state
└── Until you reach waking (most folded)
```
