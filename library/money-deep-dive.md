## The History of Dollar Dominance

### 1944: Bretton Woods

After WWII, the world agreed: US dollar = global reserve currency.
- Dollar was backed by gold ($35/ounce)
- Other currencies pegged to the dollar
- US became the center of the financial universe

**Why the US?** They had the gold (from WWII), the military, and the industrial capacity. Winner takes all.

### 1971: Nixon Shock

The US was spending on Vietnam + social programs. More dollars printed than gold to back them. Countries started demanding gold for their dollars (France especially).

Nixon's response: "We're not giving you gold anymore."

Dollar became pure fiat â€” backed by nothing physical. The world had no choice but to accept it because everything was already priced in dollars.
