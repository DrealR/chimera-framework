## HOW TO USE THIS DOCUMENT

For each scenario:
1. **READ** the situation carefully
2. **PAUSE** - Don't look at the answer yet
3. **IDENTIFY** the triality structure yourself:
   - What is Pole A?
   - What is Pole B?
   - What is The Between (the third option, the transformation, the hidden truth)?
4. **CHECK** your answer against the provided one
5. **NOTE** the insight - this builds your pattern library

**The goal is not to memorize answers. The goal is to train your recognition.**

# QUICK REFERENCE: THE RECOGNITION KEYS

When you see/hear any of these, immediately look for the hidden between:

1. **"Either... or..."** - What's the third option?
2. **"You have to choose"** - Who says? What if you don't?
3. **"There's no middle ground"** - There almost always is
4. **"Pick a side"** - Refuse the frame
5. **"Black and white"** - Look for gray (and colors)
6. **"All or nothing"** - Find the something
7. **"You can't have both"** - Really? Says who?
8. **"It's simple"** - It's probably not
9. **"The only options are..."** - Create new options
10. **"If you really X, you'd Y"** - X and Y are separate
