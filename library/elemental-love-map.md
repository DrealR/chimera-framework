## EARTH (Jesus)

**Element:** Ground, stone, stability

**Gift:** SEEING. Earth signs see reality clearly. They're stable, reliable, practical. They ground everyone around them. Observation Haki is their nature.

**Shadow:** RIGIDITY. They become walls. Immovable. Exclusive. They stop the flow. They exclude what doesn't fit their structure. Judgmental. Closed.

**Correction needed:** AIR (Aang). Release. Let go. Not everything needs to be solid. Flow. Trust. The ground can let the seed break through.

**The move:** Jesus opens the tomb. Moves the stone. Breaks the rigid exclusion. "Let everyone in."
