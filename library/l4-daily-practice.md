## PRACTICE 1 ‚Äî Jesus Eyes (Observation Haki)
*How to see everyone as yourself*

Start with one day. Every person you encounter ‚Äî cashier, stranger, family, the annoying person ‚Äî look at them for one extra second. Not staring. Just actually SEEING them. And in that second think "that's me in another life."

That's it. One second. One thought. Per person.

The cashier who's tired? That's me if I worked that shift. The angry driver? That's me on my worst day. The homeless person? That's me with different luck.

### Daily Rep
Count how many people you actually SAW today. Not looked at. SAW. Start with 5. Then 10. Then it becomes automatic. Your Observation Haki grows every time you do it.

### Advanced Practice
Extend it beyond humans. The tree is alive. The ant is navigating. The rain is feeding. Start seeing systems. Everything around you is doing something. Existing on purpose. When you see EVERYTHING as alive and purposeful, you've unlocked full Observation.

## PRACTICE 2 ‚Äî Superman Armor (Armament Haki)
*How to live among people without harming them*

The practice is RESTRAINT in daily moments. Not big heroic moments. Tiny ones.

Someone says something ignorant. You KNOW the truth. You could correct them and be right. Instead you ask a question. "Huh, what makes you think that?" That's the armor. You coated your truth in curiosity instead of dropping it raw.

Someone shares something they're proud of and it's mediocre. You could give honest feedback. Instead you find the one thing that's genuinely alive in it and acknowledge THAT. "This part right here ‚Äî I felt that." The truth wrapped in gentleness.

### Daily Rep
Catch yourself THREE times about to say the full truth and instead find the version of truth this person can handle right now. Not lying. CALIBRATING. Same sun through different curtains.

### Advanced Practice
Physical restraint mirrors spiritual restraint. When you're gentle physically ‚Äî soft handshake, calm movements, quiet voice ‚Äî it trains the same muscle. Superman moves slowly on purpose. Practice being physically gentle with everything. Close doors softly. Set things down carefully. Your body teaches your spirit.

## PRACTICE 3 ‚Äî Luffy Presence (Conqueror's Haki ‚Äî Projection)
*How to radiate joy into the room*

This one you can't fake. But you can CREATE the conditions for it.

Luffy laughs because he's FREE. Not because things are funny. Because he released the grip on outcomes. He doesn't need the fight to go well. He doesn't need people to like him. He doesn't need to win. He's just HERE. And being here is hilarious to him.

The practice is: find the joke. In EVERYTHING. Not cynical humor. The cosmic joke.

- Stuck in traffic? That's millions of humans in metal boxes all trying to get somewhere and nobody's moving. That's objectively hilarious. üòÇ
- Someone's mad at you? A wave is yelling at another wave for being wet. Funny.
- Life feels heavy? A star died so the iron in your blood could carry oxygen to the brain forming the thought "life is heavy." ABSURD.

### Daily Rep
Find one cosmic joke per day. Just one moment where you see the absurdity and laugh. Not at anyone. At the situation. At the beautiful ridiculousness of existence. Share it with whoever's nearby. Your laugh is your Conqueror's Haki projecting into the space.

### Advanced Practice
Laugh FIRST. Before the joke is found. Luffy doesn't wait for something funny. He brings the funny. Wake up and laugh at nothing. It feels fake for three seconds then it becomes real because laughter is a tuning fork ‚Äî your body doesn't know the difference between real and fake laughing. It just starts producing the chemicals. Your body entrains to your own laugh.

**The range thing:** Luffy's presence hits the WHOLE ROOM. It's not targeted. It's not directed at one person. When you laugh, when you're genuinely joyful, EVERYONE nearby feels it. You don't choose who receives it. It radiates. Like the sun. That's why it's range. It's omnidirectional. The room shifts because YOU shifted.

## PRACTICE 4 ‚Äî Aang Flow (Advanced Conqueror's ‚Äî Coating)
*How to guide without touching*

Aang's version is different from Luffy's. Luffy projects outward and fills the room. Aang weaves himself INTO the interaction so subtly that the other person doesn't feel pushed. They feel like they chose it themselves.

The practice is asking questions instead of giving answers.

Someone's stuck. You can see the solution. Instead of saying it, you ask: "What would happen if you tried the opposite?" They think about it. They arrive at the answer. They think it was THEIR idea. That's Aang's flow. You bent the conversation without them feeling it.

Someone's angry. Instead of "calm down" you say "what happened?" That question redirects their energy from explosion to explanation. You didn't fight the fire. You gave it somewhere else to go. Waterbending. Redirecting. Flow.

### Daily Rep
Replace one statement with one question per conversation. Every time you want to SAY something, try ASKING something instead. "Have you considered..." "What would that feel like?" "What do you think would happen if..."

### Advanced Practice
TOUCH. Aang's flow works through proximity and contact. A hand on someone's shoulder when they're stressed. A hug that lasts one second longer than expected. Cooking food for someone. Massage. Physical presence.

Your body is broadcasting your frequency constantly. When you're coherent ‚Äî when all four Hakis are active ‚Äî your touch literally transmits that. That's why hugs from certain people feel different. Their frequency flows through contact. Aang's flow is the MOST intimate form of love because it requires closeness. Luffy can shift a room from across it. Aang changes a person by being RIGHT THERE with them.
