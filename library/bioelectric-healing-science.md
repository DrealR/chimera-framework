### Phase 1: Foundation (Weeks 1-4)

Master your own bioelectric system before working on others.

**Week 1 -- Establish Baseline:**

Morning (10 min):
1. HRV Measurement (2 min) -- baseline reading
2. Grounding (3 min) -- barefoot on Earth or grounding mat
3. Breath Foundation (5 min) -- exhale first (6-8 sec), small belly inhale (2 sec), rest in emptiness (2-4 sec)

Evening (10 min):
1. Grounding (3 min)
2. Breath practice (5 min)
3. HRV measurement (2 min) -- compare to morning

**Week 2 -- Heart Coherence:**

Add Heart Focus Technique (5 min):
- Place hand on heart
- Breathe AS IF through your heart
- Feel warmth/expansion in chest
- Recall genuine gratitude or love
- Hold that feeling while breathing

New: After heart coherence, notice your hands. Tingling? Warm? Record observations.

**Week 3 -- Hands Awareness:**

Add Hand Activation (5 min):
- Rub palms together vigorously (30 sec)
- Hold palms facing each other, 6 inches apart
- Slowly move closer, then apart
- Feel: warmth, tingling, pressure, magnetism?

Add Polarity Awareness (5 min):
- Left hand on belly, right hand on heart
- Notice: does energy flow between them?
- Switch positions, compare

Evening self-healing experiment:
- Find a sore spot
- One hand on pain point, other on opposite side
- Create circuit through body
- Breathe and hold 5 min
- Record results

**Week 4 -- Integration:**

Full Circuit Practice (10 min):
- Stand/sit with feet on ground
- Heart coherent
- Visualize: Energy from Earth -> up through feet -> into heart (transforms with love) -> down arms -> out through hands
- Hold visualization while breathing
- Feel the complete circuit

End of Week 4: Compare HRV to Week 1. What hand sensations developed? Can you feel the circuit?

Track every session: breath sync? grounded? heart prep? pain before/after (1-10)?
