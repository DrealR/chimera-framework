# The Science Trap

*Science is good. Scientism is the trap. And how to speak every language.*

---

## Science vs Scientism

```
SCIENCE IS GOOD:
- Describes HOW things work
- Measurable, testable, improvable
- Gave us medicine, technology, understanding

SCIENCE IS LIMITED:
- Cannot answer WHY
- Cannot measure meaning
- Cannot prove love exists (but it does)
- Cannot see the 95% invisible reality
- Cannot cross the is-ought gap

THE TRAP (SCIENTISM):
"If science can't measure it, it's not real"
→ Love? Not measurable. Dismissed.
→ Meaning? Not measurable. Dismissed.
→ God? Not measurable. Dismissed.
```

**This is Smith's worldview:** "Everything is cause and effect." Neo's answer: "Because I choose to."
