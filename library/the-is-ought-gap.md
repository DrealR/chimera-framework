## The Gap Science Cannot Cross

Professor Jiang revealed a fundamental limit:

**Science can tell you consequences of actions. Science cannot tell you if actions are right.**

| What Science CAN Do | What Science CANNOT Do |
|--------------------|------------------------|
| Tell you consequences | Tell you if consequences are worth it |
| Map brain activity during sacrifice | Tell you if sacrifice is worthy |
| Predict outcomes | Rank outcomes in moral order |
| Describe mechanisms | Prescribe values |
| Calculate probability of winning | Tell you if fighting is meaningful |

This is the IS-OUGHT gap:
- IS = what is true (science's domain)
- OUGHT = what should be done (conscience's domain)

**No amount of data crosses this gap.**
