# Plato's Cave: The Master Allegory

*The cave is the mind. The chains are beliefs. The shadows are perceptions. The sun is Love.*

---

## The Allegory

```
Prisoners chained in a cave since birth.
They face a wall.
Behind them: a fire.
Between fire and prisoners: people carrying objects.
Prisoners see only SHADOWS of objects on wall.
They think shadows ARE reality.
They name the shadows, study them, become experts.
One prisoner is freed.
Turns around - sees the fire (blinding at first).
Climbs out of cave - sees the sun (MORE blinding).
Eventually adjusts - sees REAL objects.
Realizes: shadows were never reality.
Returns to tell others.
They think he's CRAZY.
They might even KILL him for disturbing their "reality."
```

STAGE 6: RETURNING

- Some turn around
- The pattern spreads
