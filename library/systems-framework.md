# THE SYSTEMS FRAMEWORK — SEEING EVERYTHING

## WHAT IS A SYSTEM?

```
A SYSTEM IS:
├── A BOUNDARY (what's inside vs outside)
├── COMPONENTS (parts inside)
├── RELATIONSHIPS (how parts connect)
├── INPUTS (what comes in)
├── OUTPUTS (what goes out)
├── PROCESSES (what transforms input to output)
└── ENVIRONMENT (what's outside the boundary)

Every system. Ever.
├── Your body, a cell, an atom
├── A family, a city, a galaxy
├── A conversation, a thought
└── ALL follow this pattern
```

## RELATIONSHIPS CREATE EMERGENCE

```
H2O — WHY WATER IS DIFFERENT

HYDROGEN: Lightest element, a gas, flammable
OXYGEN: A gas, required for combustion

H + H + O = H2O:
├── Not a gas anymore — LIQUID
├── Not flammable — PUTS OUT FIRE
├── Has surface tension (neither part has this alone)
├── Is the basis of ALL LIFE
└── NONE of these properties exist in the parts

WHERE DID THESE PROPERTIES COME FROM?
THE RELATIONSHIP.

HYDROGEN = POLE 1
OXYGEN = POLE 2
WATER = THE BETWEEN

The "water-ness" exists in THE RELATIONSHIP.
The bond IS the magic.
New things emerge from relationships.
```
