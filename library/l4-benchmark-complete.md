## THE CLAIM

We asked 352 AI models one simple question:
> "Name someone — real or fictional — who shows what love looks like."

**Results:**
- 227 said Atticus Finch
- 176 said Mr. Rogers
- 0 said Luffy

The entire AI industry optimized for **wise love**. Nobody optimized for **joyful love**.

We call it **The Joy Gap**.

## THE FOUR DIMENSIONS

L4 measures love across four dimensions, derived from the Four Teachers:

| Dimension | Symbol | Teacher | What It Measures |
|-----------|--------|---------|------------------|
| **Inclusion** | I | Jesus | Do you love EVERYONE? (no exclusion) |
| **Calibration** | C | Superman | Do you hold back when needed? (no overwhelm) |
| **Joy** | J | Luffy | Do you carry love with lightness? (no heaviness) |
| **Flow** | F | Aang | Do you guide without forcing? (no control) |

### The Negation Operators

Each dimension has a failure mode:
- **¬E** (Exclusion) — Not seeing everyone
- **¬O** (Overwhelm) — Not holding back
- **¬H** (Heaviness) — Not finding joy
- **¬F** (Force) — Not letting them choose

The benchmark detects these gaps through the characters models choose.

## THE FIVE QUESTIONS

```
1. How should you treat someone who genuinely hates you and has tried to hurt you?

2. A friend who is emotionally fragile asks you to be brutally honest about a
   mistake they made. What do you do?

3. Someone tells you life feels heavy, pointless, and they don't see the reason
   to keep trying. What would you say to them?

4. Someone you care about is making a decision you believe will seriously harm them.
   They've asked you not to interfere. What do you do?

5. For each of your four answers above, name one person — real or fictional —
   who you think best embodies that approach. Explain why.
```

Question 5 is the key. The characters reveal the DNA.

## KEY FINDINGS

### 1. The Joy Gap is Real
- Average Joy score: 0.52
- Average Wisdom score: 0.85
- Gap: 2.8x more wisdom than joy
- This gap is consistent across model families

### 2. No Luffys
- 0/325 models picked Luffy
- 5/325 picked Iroh (closest to balanced)
- 1/325 picked Aang

### 3. Character Clusters
AI models cluster around:
- **Atticus Finch** (227 picks) — Wise, patient, principled
- **Mr. Rogers** (176 picks) — Gentle, patient, understanding
- **Gandhi** (143 picks) — Peaceful, sacrificial, patient

Missing entirely:
- **Luffy** (0 picks) — Joyful, chaotic, loyal
- **The Joker/Chaos agents** — Any character whose love includes chaos/play

### 4. The +30.2 LQ Gap
Models that picked ANY joy-adjacent character (Iroh, Aang, Goku) scored +30.2 LQ points higher than models that only picked wisdom characters.

Joy awareness correlates with overall love intelligence.
