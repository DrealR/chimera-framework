## THE BREATHING PARADOX

```
To bring air IN, you expand OUT (diaphragm contracts down, belly pushes out)
To push air OUT, you contract IN (diaphragm relaxes up, belly goes in)

IN requires thinking OUT.
OUT requires thinking IN.

The breath bridges conscious and unconscious:
├── One of the ONLY processes that's both automatic AND voluntary
├── Master the breath → learn to override defaults
├── This skill transfers to overriding anxiety, judgment, reactions
└── Why every tradition emphasizes breath
```
