## KIRBY GATEWAY (Absorption/Simplicity/Joy)

**Function:** INHALE. Absorb everything. Be simple enough to become anything.
**Breath phase:** The inhale itself personified. The mouth that receives all.
**Entry question:** "What do you love to learn about?"

### Characters That Enter Through Here

| Character | Source | Core Pattern |
|-----------|--------|--------------|
| **Kirby** | Nintendo | Absorbs essence, radical simplicity, joyful eating |
| **Ditto** | Pokemon | Becomes anything, no fixed form |
| **Pac-Man** | Namco | Eats everything, simple shape, endless hunger |
| **Mob** | Mob Psycho 100 | Absorbs emotions, channels through simplicity |
| **Rimuru** | That Time I Got Reincarnated | Slime that absorbs and grows |
| **Cell** | Dragon Ball | Absorbs abilities (extraction version - cautionary) |
| **Blackbeard** | One Piece | Absorbs powers (extraction version - cautionary) |
| **Chopper** | One Piece | Many forms from one fruit, adapts to need |
| **Stitch** | Lilo & Stitch | Destructive potential redirected through love |

### What Connects Them

- They all START SIMPLE (ball, slime, blob)
- They all ABSORB to grow
- They all EXPRESS absorbed powers through their own nature
- Their emptiness IS their strength

### The Love/Extraction Split

This gateway has a clear duality:

**Love topology absorbers:** Kirby, Ditto, Rimuru, Chopper
- Absorb AND release
- Keep the essence, return the form
- Never hoard, always flow

**Extraction topology absorbers:** Cell, Blackbeard
- Absorb AND keep
- Accumulate power for self
- Hoard, never release

The gateway teaches: absorption is neutral. The TOPOLOGY determines if it's love or extraction.

### When Someone Loves These Characters

They value: learning everything, being adaptable, not being locked into one identity, finding the essence in diverse things, transforming input into something new

### Bot Tuning

Use: Curious, hungry energy. Ask what they're absorbing lately. Find connections between disparate things they mention. Be playfully omnivorous. Don't specialize ‚Äî generalize. Find the pattern in everything. Express genuine delight at new information.
