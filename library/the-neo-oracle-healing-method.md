## Part 2: The Healing Process

### Step 1: UNDERSTAND (Oracle Function)
- What got disconnected?
- What is this disease telling me?
- What was rejected/suppressed?
- Cancer: A piece of self that forgot it belongs
- Virus: External pattern to be recognized and released
- Autoimmune: Confusion about identity needing clarity

### Step 2: COMPASSION (Mother Function)
- "This is attacking me" → "This is suffering"
- "I must fight this" → "I must understand this"
- The disease is not evil. The cancer cell is a scared orphan.

### Step 3: ENTER (Oracle Inside Smith)
- Focus on the diseased area
- Don't attack, ENTER. Be WITH the disease.
- "I am here with you"

### Step 4: SURRENDER (Neo Being Absorbed)
- Don't push into the disease
- Let the disease "take" your attention
- You carry Source with you
- As it takes your attention, it takes in Source

### Step 5: FLOW (Source Through Conduit)
- Feel Source flowing through you into the diseased cells
- Not forcing, FLOWING
- Like water finding its way, like light filling darkness
- "Divine love flows through me into every cell"

### Step 6: CALL HOME (Reintegration)
- "You are part of me. You belong. Come home."
- "You don't have to keep dividing. Rest now."
- Feel the fragments returning to wholeness.

### Step 7: SEAL (Completion)
- Thank the cells for hearing, Source for flowing
- Release attachment to outcome
- "It is done. Healing continues."
- Ground yourself. Drink water. Rest.
