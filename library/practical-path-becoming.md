## Phase 1: Foundation (Now - 3 Months)

### Morning Practice (15-20 min)

```
1. BREATH (5 min)
   └── Exhale first
   └── Long exhale (6-8 sec)
   └── Small inhale (natural)
   └── Rest in emptiness
   └── This builds your "steel"

2. QI GONG (5-10 min)
   └── YouTube: "Qi Gong for beginners"
   └── Breath + movement
   └── Energy flowing

3. INTENTION (2 min)
   └── "Today I give first"
   └── "I am a channel, not a container"
   └── "I receive pain, transform with love, release healing"
   └── Connect to Source (prayer)
```

### Throughout Day

```
4. PRESENCE IN CONVERSATIONS
   └── Every person = Practice
   └── Listen fully (exhale attention)
   └── Make them feel heard
   └── Don't show off what you know
   └── Ask about THEM

5. BREATH AWARENESS
   └── While driving: Exhale first
   └── In traffic: 3 Honeydew breaths at red lights
   └── When stressed: One long exhale
   └── Always return to breath

6. READ THE FLOW
   └── Traffic, people, situations
   └── Notice frequencies
   └── Practice seeing patterns
```

### Evening Practice (15-20 min)

```
7. SINGING (5-10 min)
   └── One song minimum
   └── Breath training
   └── Expression
   └── Joy

8. GUITAR (10 min)
   └── Learn one new thing
   └── Or practice what you know
   └── Slow, intentional

9. TONGLEN PRACTICE (5 min)
   └── Sit quietly
   └── Think of someone suffering
   └── Breathe IN their pain (visualize dark smoke)
   └── Transform in heart (love)
   └── Breathe OUT healing (visualize light)
   └── 5-10 cycles
   └── This trains pain transformation

10. RELEASE (2 min)
    └── "I release what is not mine"
    └── Shake hands
    └── Let go of the day
    └── Sleep from peace
```

## Singing + Breath: Technical Guide

### Meditation Breath (Honeydew)

```
EXHALE FIRST: 6-8 seconds (slow, complete)
    ↓
INHALE: 1-2 seconds (SMALL, into BELLY, quick)
    ↓
REST: 2-4 seconds (empty, don't rush)
    ↓
REPEAT

THE INHALE:

├── Not a big GASP (chest breathing, shallow)
├── Quick drop into BELLY (diaphragm)
├── 1-2 seconds
├── Feels like belly pops out slightly
├── NOT chest rising up
├── Efficient, not effortful
└── Like a quick sip, not a huge gulp
```

### Singing Breath

```
1. QUICK INHALE (Before phrase)
   ├── 1-2 seconds
   ├── Into BELLY (diaphragm)
   ├── Belly expands, shoulders stay down
   └── This is your "fuel"

2. SING (The exhale)
   ├── The phrase IS your exhale
   ├── Air passes over vocal cords
   ├── Controlled release
   ├── Support from belly (not throat)
   └── As long as the phrase needs

3. CATCH BREATH (Between phrases)
   ├── Quick inhale at natural pauses
   ├── Don't rush
   ├── Belly, not chest
   └── Prepare for next phrase
```

### Singing as Triality

```
POLE 1: Too much inhale → Gasping, tense, strained
POLE 2: Too little inhale → Running out, weak, breathy
THE BETWEEN: Efficient breath → Smooth, controlled, effortless

Same with life:
├── Too much taking → Hoarding → Stuck
├── Too little taking → Depleted → Empty
├── Just enough → Flow → Mastery
└── The between, always
```
