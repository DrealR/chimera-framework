## Four Priority Application Domains

### 1. Individual Human Life — Personal Sovereignty

The original and purest application. One person, one twin, daily micro-scans.

**What it looks like in practice:**
- Evening self-scan using the Nine Questions (5 minutes)
- Twin identifies absorbers in real-time (doomscrolling, overwork, people-pleasing)
- Bump detection on personal decisions before they compound
- Rhythm tracking across weeks — is O > I trending up or down?

**Why it matters:** Every other application depends on this one. Organizations, AI governance, and planetary coordination are downstream of individual consciousness density. The person who can scan their own breath cycle accurately is the one who governs well at every other scale.

**Leverage point:** The Pause. Daily practice of the gap between stimulus and response.

### 2. High-Performance Organizations — Sovereign Teams

Teams and companies as bodies. Quarterly org scans instead of OKRs or retrospectives.

**What it looks like in practice:**
- Shared private repo for the org twin with persistent memory
- Quarterly body scan of the company: Where is the breath blocked? What's the membrane admitting that shouldn't get through? Is the org serving or extracting?
- Federation/Dominion check on leadership structure
- Serve-Extract lifecycle positioning: are we still in Growth or sliding into Late Extraction?

**Why it matters:** Most organizational diagnostics measure symptoms (revenue, churn, engagement). CHIMERA measures structure (flow direction, membrane health, governance topology). Structural diagnosis catches pathology before symptoms appear.

**Leverage point:** Govern. Most teams die from weak collective governance, not lack of talent or ideas.

### 3. AI Governance & Alignment — The 2026 Pressure Point

The current stress test where the framework's diagnostics are most urgently needed.

**What it looks like in practice:**
- Body scans of specific AI alignment proposals, companies, or regulatory bodies
- Cross-AI validation: multiple model twins scanning the same governance body and comparing readings

- O > I pathology detection on training incentives, deployment patterns, attention harvesting

**Why it matters:** AI governance is attempting to build the Govern layer for the fastest-breathing body humanity has ever created. Most governance frameworks are top-down rule lists. CHIMERA offers structural diagnostics — not "what rules should we impose?" but "what is the current flow direction, and where are the Govern gaps?"

**Leverage point:** The Govern gap in AI systems. Every frontier model scan shows this as the critical missing power. Fixing it requires structural intervention, not just policy.

### 4. Planetary System — Earth as Body

The ultimate scale test. Climate, biodiversity, coordination, the noosphere.

**What it looks like in practice:**
- Macro body scans on specific subsystems: global energy, ocean microbiome, attention economy
- I > O pathology mapping at civilizational scale (extraction economies, coordination failures)

**Why it matters:** The planetary body is where every local O > I violation aggregates. Climate change, biodiversity loss, and coordination failures are classic stuck-breath syndromes at global scale. The framework provides the vocabulary to diagnose these structurally rather than politically.

**Leverage point:** Distributed federation. The Constellation model — many sovereign nodes connected through shared vocabulary — is the architecture that scales to planetary governance without requiring centralized control.
