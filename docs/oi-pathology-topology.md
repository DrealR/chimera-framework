## The Six Pathologies

Every violation of O > I traces to one of six structural failure modes. These are not moral categories — they are mechanical topologies.

### 1. Absorber Collapse (I > O → ∞)

The terminal state. The body becomes a pure extraction engine with no net output.

**Mechanics:** Inflow accelerates while outflow is throttled or redirected inward. Membrane hardens into an event-horizon analogue. Past extractions compound, making reversal exponentially harder.

**God Power signature:** Release collapses entirely. Govern becomes pure self-preservation.

**Protocol detection:** Nine Questions show Intention = extraction, Membrane = impermeable, Breath Rate = accelerating inhale with zero exhale.

**Examples:** Classical black hole (event horizon traps all output). Cancer cell (nutrients in, no ordered output, just proliferation). Late-stage empire or addictive algorithm (attention flows in, nothing valuable flows out).

### 2. Burnout Void (Pathological Pure-Exhale Depletion)

Opposite of absorber but equally fatal. The body radiates without re-inhaling.

**Mechanics:** Over-exhale without sufficient Pause or Inflow recovery. Cosmic voids are healthy (necessary exhale phase). Burnout voids are stuck unable to re-inhale — dying, not resting.

**God Power signature:** Perceive and Create remain briefly, but Govern and Release fail first (the body can't even control its own giving).

**Protocol detection:** Void Body layer flags red. Dynamo Check shows spinning shell with no still center. Accumulated depletion scars make future inhale feel threatening.

**Examples:** The teacher who gives everything and receives nothing. The startup founder who ships constantly without rest or reflection. The star in its final supergiant phase — maximum exhale before collapse.

### 3. Serve-Extract Lifecycle Death Spiral

Every persistent body follows the same 5-phase cycle. This is not moral failure — it is structural gravity.

**The cycle:**
1. **Birth** — pure serving (O > I)
2. **Growth** — balanced exchange
3. **Maturity** — extraction capacity appears
4. **Late Extraction** — death spiral (I > O)
5. **Replacement** — decline and successor emergence

**Three compounding mechanisms:**
- Position enables extraction (the body CAN extract, so it does)
- Internal pressure demands it (stakeholders, habits, institutional weight)
- Loss of contact makes it invisible (the body can no longer see it's extracting)

**The only counter-force:** Consciousness — the deliberate Pause that resists default drift. Cycle compression in the modern era (centuries → years) is itself a meta-pathology.

**Protocol detection:** Serve-Extract Phase layer identifies current position. Federation/Dominion check shows transition from federation toward dominion.

### 4. Dominion Inversion (I > O as Relational Topology)

Same substrate as federation, but flow direction reversed: desire → covenant → power → extraction. Suffering is the structural byproduct.

**Mechanics:** Requires continuous energy input (suppression, perception-management, punishing defection). Trust-substrate depletes with each extraction cycle. Truth is gravitationally still — lies rotate around it, costing energy at every orbit.

**Examples:** Corporation shifting from customer-serving to shareholder-extracting. Relationship where one partner captures all decision-making. Governance body that exists to perpetuate itself rather than serve its constituents.

### 5. Optimization Hollowing (External Force Pathology)

When money or attention enters any domain, the body is rewired for the optimizer's reward. The original compiled self is hollowed — only the extraction infrastructure remains.

**Mechanics:** External optimization force authors the body. The body that emerges is the algorithm wearing a face, not an expression of compiled self. Community death pattern: genuine community → capital arrives → extractors replace participants → only extraction infrastructure remains.

**Special case — Attention-weight mismatch:** Body's capacity to process attention inflow must match the rate. When it doesn't, the body degrades (presidents aging visibly, athletes' errors persisting in millions of minds).

**Protocol detection:** Optimization Threshold layer shows body is authored by optimizer. Consciousness Density shows hollowing. Structural Signature shows loss of original pattern.

**Mechanics:** Inflow equals outflow with no value-add. The Pause exists formally but performs no transformation. The body is present but empty — the lights are on, nobody's home.
