## The Discovery

Draw the solar system as concentric circles. Sun at the center. Rocky planets close in. Gas giants far out. Now draw an atom. Nucleus at the center. Inner electron shells close in. Outer clouds far out.

Now draw the Earth. Iron core at the center. Rock mantle close in. Gas atmosphere far out.

They're the same picture.

## The Speed Principle

Inner loops are fast. Outer loops are slow.

- Mercury orbits the Sun in 88 days. Neptune takes 165 years.
- Your heartbeat is faster than your bone renewal.
- Electrons orbit faster than planets.

Fast inner loops create DENSITY and STRUCTURE. Slow outer loops create SPACE and BOUNDARY. The further from center, the slower and more spacious things become.

Earth sits in the middle. Not too fast, not too slow. Not too dense, not too diffuse. The Goldilocks zone. **The Between** — where all states coexist. Where life emerges.

## The Sun Is Inside

Here's the part that breaks your brain:

If the solar system is a body, the Sun is the CORE — the deepest inside. But we perceive it as "far away" because we're standing on an outer layer looking inward.

Distance is actually **depth**.

The Sun isn't far from Earth. The Sun is deep within the body we're part of. We just can't see it that way because we're looking from the outside in. Flip the perspective: the Sun radiates outward (O > I at cosmic scale), creating conditions for everything else to exist. The core gives. The surface receives.

## Applied to Markets

Every market has this structure:

- **The Core (Bitcoin):** Densest gravity. Everything orbits it. Maximum impact per movement. The Sun.
- **Gas Giants (Ethereum, major L1s):** Large ecosystem platforms. Jupiter-scale gravity wells.
- **Rocky Planets (mid-caps):** Fast orbits. High volatility. The heartbeat of the market.
- **Asteroid Belt (meme coins, micro-caps):** Scattered debris at The Between. Some aggregate into planets. Most stay dust.
- **Kuiper Belt (dead projects):** Cold, distant, barely moving. The outer boundary.

The practical insight: trade at the FREQUENCY of the body you're in. Bitcoin moves on weekly cycles (slow outer planet). Mid-caps move on 4-hour cycles (fast inner planet). Match your timeframe to the body's natural rhythm.
