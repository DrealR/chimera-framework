## Loop Mathematics

Every loop — at any layer — can be described by four numbers:

- **Frequency:** How fast it cycles
- **Amplitude:** How big each cycle is
- **Phase:** Where in the cycle it currently is
- **Coupling Strength:** How much it influences neighboring loops

A healthy heart has a frequency of ~72 beats/minute, an amplitude measured in blood pressure, a phase you can feel in your pulse, and coupling strength that synchronizes with your breathing, walking, and emotional state.

A market candle has a frequency (timeframe), amplitude (range), phase (where in the trend), and coupling strength (correlation with other assets).

Same math. Different body.
