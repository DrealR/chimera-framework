### Shannon's Core Insight

Information IS surprise. A message that tells you something you already know contains ZERO information. A message that tells you something unexpected contains MAXIMUM information. Information = the reduction of uncertainty.

This is why body scans are powerful: when you tell someone "you're a gardener" and they've never thought of themselves that way, the information content is HIGH.
