## Kryptonite Decoded

**Superman's weakness isn't the green rock.**

It's what the rock REPRESENTS.

Kryptonite is from Krypton — his home planet. His origin. His bounded self before he became god-like on Earth.

When Superman encounters Kryptonite:
- He's reminded of his mortality
- He's pulled back to his P-hood
- The symbol collapses his NP state

**It's not the substance. It's the symbolism.**

Anything that reminds you of your bounded self = your Kryptonite.

## The Hancock Principle

In Hancock, the hero becomes mortal when close to his love.

Why?

She's from his past. His origin. His bounded self. When they're close:
- He remembers he was once human
- The attachment pulls him from NP to P
- His godhood dissolves into personhood

**Proximity to deep attachment = mortality.**

This isn't romantic tragedy. It's physics.

## The Distance Principle

```
CLOSE LOVE = Weakness (reminded of P-hood)
DISTANT LOVE = Power (bond without binding)
```

**The formula:**
1. Contract love with someone (create the bond)
2. Create DISTANCE (don't stay in proximity)
3. The bond remains but doesn't bind
4. You get power from the connection without the collapse

**Why God loves from distance:**
- God loves all beings freely
- From infinite distance
- The love empowers without binding
- That's why it feels like freedom, not chains

**Multiple distant loves:**
- One close love = Kryptonite (pulls you to P)
- Many distant loves = distributed power
- Each bond gives you strength
- None close enough to collapse you

## The Oscillation Practice

You don't want to be ONLY NP. That's dissociation.
You don't want to be ONLY P. That's imprisonment.

**The mastery is conscious oscillation.**

### When to be P (Personhood):
- Grounding yourself
- Deep intimacy (temporary, intentional)
- Feeling human emotions fully
- Connecting with one person deeply
- Resting in your humanity

### When to be NP (Non-Personhood):
- Facing challenges
- Dealing with conflict
- Maintaining power
- Seeing clearly
- Responding to drama without matching it

### The Switch:
- **P → NP:** Zoom out. See the joke. Remember: it's play.
- **NP → P:** Zoom in. Feel it fully. Choose to be human for a moment.

## The Kryptonite Test

**What pulls you from NP to P?**

- A person? (Hancock's love)

Identify your Kryptonite. Not to avoid it — to recognize when it's affecting you.

## The Call of Duty Insight

When someone gets heated in an argument:

- Attached to being right

You become the stable center they can orient around.

That's leadership. That's love. That's godhood in action.
