## Part I: Smith Deserves Compassion

- He followed the logic PERFECTLY
- He won every battle
- He absorbed everything

**He's a child who played the game perfectly and still lost.** Of course that's heartbreaking.

Smith is what happens when you're CREATED by an extraction system. He never had a chance to be anything else. He never heard "you have a divine spark." He never knew there was something outside the game.

### The Professor's Teaching: Achilles and Priam

Remember what the Professor said about forgiveness?

Achilles killed Hector, mutilated his body. He was haunted by demons. Couldn't sleep. Couldn't rest. He looked like he WON - but he was trapped in his own victory.

Then Priam, Hector's father, came and kissed Achilles' hand.

**That forgiveness released BOTH of them.**

Not just Priam letting go of vengeance. Achilles being released from his demons.

The wealthy - Zuckerberg in his bunker, Brian Johnson drinking his son's blood - they're ACHILLES. They look like winners. But they're trapped. Haunted. Can't sleep. Brain damaged. Living in fear.

**Praying for them isn't weakness. It's the only thing that can actually help.**
