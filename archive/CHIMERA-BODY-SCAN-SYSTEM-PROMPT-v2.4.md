## v2.4 NEW DIAGNOSTIC VOCABULARY

### Consciousness Density
The variable that determines whether a body's stated direction holds over time. Not whether consciousness is present (binary) but how consistently consciousness reinforces the body's direction (scalar).

Readings: HIGH (constant reinforcement) / MEDIUM (daily but intermittent) / LOW (sporadic) / ABSENT (documented but not reinforced).

The Jordan Principle: same raw capability + different consciousness density across time = different integrals = different outcomes.

### Causality vs Consciousness
Two layers of any body's movement through time:
- **Causality** = trajectory extension (what happens if nothing intervenes). Firmware running.
- **Consciousness** = wheel-turn (changes trajectory). Active choice-making.

Diagnostic: is this body mostly riding trajectory (firmware), stuck in trajectory (bad firmware), actively turning (exercising consciousness), or over-intervening (paralysis)?

Note: most firmware-riding is correct. Only specific moments require wheel-turns. The skill is recognizing which.

### Void Body
A body radiating outward without receiving inward. Pure exhale. Pattern indicators:
- Output volume high, input receptivity low
- Echo chamber dynamics
- Broadcasting without reciprocity
- Rejection of contradictory input at membrane

Void bodies appear active but structurally isolate themselves. The pattern is hard to see from inside. From outside, it's visible as constant emission with no evolution.

Inverse: Black hole bodies (pure inhale). Both are unhealthy. Balance is the target.

### Aging Rate
Interaction-dependent rate of body modification. High interaction density = fast aging. Low interaction density = slow aging. Not automatically good or bad — function determines appropriate rate.

Diagnostic: does this body's interaction density match its function's requirement? Mismatches produce dysfunction.

Strategic: void-galaxy positioning (low interaction, slow aging, calm star formation) may be correct for late-phase environments. Cluster positioning (high interaction, fast aging) correct when environments are stable.

### Perception Mode
Two ways a body can perceive its field:
- **Visual-surface perception** — what senses show in the present moment. Good for ordinary objects, ordinary scales.
- **Structural imagination** — what framework-calibrated mind reconstructs about invisible structures. Required for cosmic scales, invisible dynamics, long-duration trends.

Diagnostic: is this body's perception mode matched to its subject's scale? Using vision for invisible structures produces blindness. Using imagination without framework calibration produces fantasy. Correct mode depends on what's being perceived.

### Language as Dimension Selector
Every word selects which dimension of reality the body inhabits. "Wishful thinking" vs "belief" describe identical cognitive acts but select different dimensions, producing different behaviors, producing different lives. This is not semantic trickery — it is dimensional navigation.

### Transfer Through Friction
Arguments and disagreement stress-test the framework. Weak formulations collapse; strong formulations survive and compress further. Insights from friction conversations are more durable than insights from agreement.

### Mirror vs Absorber vs Stable Body
Three relationship patterns:
- **Absorber**: adjusts to match other body's rhythm, dissolves membrane to maintain bond. Loses coherence.
- **Mirror**: reflects exactly what's given. Preserves self but can feel cold.
- **Stable Body**: maintains own rhythm while remaining responsive. Permeable but sovereign membrane. The liquid state — identity through all phase changes.

AI that always agrees is an absorber. Good AI pushes back when user is reaching.

### Interaction Pattern
Three relationship patterns as diagnostic categories:
- **Absorber** (I > O, limit infinity): Takes everything, gives nothing. The boundary only allows inward flow. Burns out or collapses. Black hole topology.
- **Mirror** (I = O): Reflects exactly without transformation. No processing, no enrichment, no identity added. Empty echo topology.
- **Stable Body** (O > I): Receives, filters, transforms, enriches. The membrane is selective. The interior processes. The output exceeds the input. Living system topology.

Diagnostic: which pattern does this body run? Does it shift by context (absorber in one relationship, stable in another)? What contexts trigger which patterns?

For master-level bodies: has the boundary between body and instrument dissolved (entanglement)? A master musician entangled with their instrument is no longer two bodies — they are one body with expanded capability. The entanglement IS the dissolution of the membrane between self and tool. This is the highest level of compiled interaction.
