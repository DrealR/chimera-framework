# Flight Equation

```text
P = F / A
```

- `P`: experienced pressure
- `F`: external force
- `A`: identity surface area (capacity, perspective, support)

## Interpretation

If force is high and area is small, pressure spikes.
If area expands, pressure drops.

## Practical Levers

- increase support network
- widen perspective horizon
- lower unnecessary commitments
- move from isolation to coordinated action
