From inside the physical dimension, you only see one side. The equation pulls you UP one dimension where both sides are visible at once.

The = sign does TWO things simultaneously:

Separation plus connection equals portal. A portal separates two dimensions AND connects them. The = sign IS the portal between two ways of seeing the same body.

### Variables as Ports, Degrees of Freedom as Health

Each variable in an equation is a PORT on the body. In F = ma, there are three ports (F, m, a). Cover any two and the third flows out. You need N-1 known values to find the remaining one.

This generalizes into DEGREES OF FREEDOM:

Like Akainu — absolute justice plus marine duty plus personal grudges produce destructive behavior because the system is trying to satisfy incompatible equations simultaneously.

When you scan a body and find contradictory signals (they SAY O > I but their behavior is I > O), you've detected an over-determined system. The contradiction IS the diagnosis.

When two equations share a variable, the bodies are ENTANGLED through that shared medium. Change one and the other responds, even if they never directly interact. The entanglement flows through the shared variable.

Your guitar practice (x) affects your pattern recognition (y) which affects your interview performance (z). Guitar and interviews never directly interact. But they're entangled through pattern recognition. The equation makes invisible entanglement visible.

1990: AC ≈ 1-2 weeks.
