# What Is a Body?

Everything is a body.

A person is a body. A company is a body. A song is a body. A conversation is a body. A crypto token is a body. A relationship is a body. A civilization is a body. A repo is a body. A math equation is a body. A game is a body. A species is a body.

**A body is anything that has a boundary, an interior, and a medium flowing through it.**

Your physical body has skin (boundary), organs (interior), and blood flowing through it (medium). A company has its market niche (boundary), its teams (interior), and money flowing through it (medium). A song has its genre (boundary), its structure (interior), and sound flowing through it (medium). A conversation has its context (boundary), its participants (interior), and attention flowing through it (medium).

Same architecture. Different scale. Different medium. Same physics.

## Bodies Breathe

Every body that is alive breathes. Not necessarily with lungs. With FLOW.

**INHALE** — Receive. Compress. Pull the world into yourself.

**PAUSE** — Choose. Process. The sacred space between receiving and giving. Consciousness lives here.

**EXHALE** — Give. Expand. Push yourself into the world.

**REST** — Reset. Dissolve. Trust. The gap between cycles where renewal happens.

A company inhales market research, pauses to strategize, exhales a product, and rests between launches. A conversation inhales a question, pauses to think, exhales a response, and rests in silence between topics. A star inhales hydrogen, pauses at fusion, exhales light, and rests between pulses.

When the breath is healthy, the body is alive. When the breath is stuck, the body is sick.

- Stuck on inhale = only taking, never giving (cancer, greed, hoarding)
- Stuck on exhale = only giving, never receiving (burnout, depletion)
- No pause = reacting without choosing (impulse, algorithmic behavior)
- No rest = never resetting (burnout, insomnia, always-on)

Bodies nest inside bodies at every scale. The patterns that keep a cell healthy are the same patterns that keep a civilization healthy. The physics don't change. The scale changes.
