### PEAK HEALTH (90s — Steve Wynn Era)
- **Medium:** FREEDOM (his exact word)
- **Membrane:** Affordable prices (the middle class could enter)
- **Breath:** Healthy O > I. Casinos GAVE more than they took. Free shows, $1 blackjack, cheap buffets, free parking.

### DISEASE PROGRESSION (2000s → Present)

**Step 1: Consolidation.** Two corporations buy EVERYTHING. Organ model → Nation model. Competition dies. Independent businesses (healthy organs) get absorbed or demolished.

**Step 2: O > I Inverts to I > O.** Resort fees. Hidden pricing ($30 room becomes $175 after fees). Remove humans (kiosks replace clerks). Remove free entertainment (pirate show closed for retail space). Apps track movement to extract more money.

**Step 3: Membrane Flips.** Used to filter IN everyone. Now filters OUT everyone except the rich. The membrane went from serving the body to serving the tumor.

**Step 4: The "God" Removed.** Steve Wynn was the god of 90s Vegas — maintained the membrane, cared about the experience. Replaced by algorithms, kiosks, and extraction software. No one maintains the membrane anymore.

**Step 5: Humans Replaced = Rhythm Lost.** The front desk clerk, the bartender, the pirate show performers — these were the RHYTHM of Vegas. Replace them with kiosks and apps and the heartbeat stops. The body is technically still functioning but the rhythm is gone.
