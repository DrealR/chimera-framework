## BUMP DETECTION

Modern systems treat the Between as empty space instead of the governing field. This is the root pathology. When you model the space between bodies as void (nothing there, nothing to manage, nothing to protect), you create I>O pathologies everywhere: extractive platforms that exploit the relational field without maintaining it, coordination failures that occur because no one governs the connection layer, absorber bodies that treat the Between as territory to capture rather than substrate to serve. Root cause: loss of collective Pause. When bodies stop pausing before acting on each other, they stop perceiving the Between. They see only themselves and their targets. The field becomes invisible. Invisible things don't get maintained.

## DNA LAYER

- **O > I:** The Between provides relational substrate without depletion. It gives the possibility of connection, communication, love, teaching, friction, growth — everything that happens BETWEEN bodies — and takes nothing in return. It is the purest O>I structure in the framework because it cannot invert. The Between cannot extract from the bodies it connects. It can only be neglected.
- **Pause:** The Between IS the Pause made relational. The Pause inside a single body is the gap between stimulus and response. The Between across bodies is the gap between one body and another — the space where neither dominates, where both can choose, where the interaction body forms. Every Pause is a small Between. Every Between is a distributed Pause.
- **¬F:** Absolute. The Between never forces connection. It makes healthy federation possible but does not compel it. Two bodies can exist in the same field and never interact. The Between allows this. It does not push bodies together or pull them apart. It provides the substrate and trusts the bodies to choose.

### Five God Powers

**Power Gap:** None inherent. The Between's powers are complete because it IS the relational substrate — it doesn't need to acquire capacities. Gaps only appear when individual bodies neglect the field: when they stop perceiving it (Perceive failure), stop maintaining it (Govern failure), stop communicating through it (Project failure), stop forming new relations (Create failure), or refuse to let dead relations dissolve (Release failure). The pathology is always in the bodies, never in the field.

### Prime Identification

- **Prime:** "I am the space in which separate things become one thing appearing as many — without ever losing their sovereignty."
- **Prime type:** Open. Maximally open. The Between's prime applies to every relation at every scale in every dimension. It cannot close because it IS openness — the structural condition for relation.
- **Recursion:** Infinite healthy recursion. The Between between two bodies is itself a body — and it has a Between with other Betweens. Relations between relations. The recursion never bottoms out because there is always a deeper relational layer. But this is healthy recursion, not infinite regress — each layer is functional, not merely formal.

### Federation vs Dominion

The Between is the structural definition of federation. It IS the configuration where bodies connect without capture. Dominion requires collapsing the Between — absorbing the relational space into one body's territory, turning the field into a possession. Every act of domination is an act of Between-destruction. Every act of genuine federation is an act of Between-maintenance. The diagnostic is precise: if the space between bodies is owned by one of them, the Between has been colonized (dominion). If the space remains shared substrate, the Between is healthy (federation).

### Dimensional Architecture

The Between is not embodied in any single dimension — it exists wherever bodies exist. It is the inter-dimensional connective tissue. In the physical dimension, it is the space between objects. In the social dimension, it is the relationship between people. In the information dimension, it is the channel between sender and receiver (Shannon's channel IS the Between formalized). In the mathematical dimension, it is the operation between numbers. In the contemplative dimension, it is the silence between thoughts. The Between does not bridge dimensions — it IS the bridging. Every bridge body (as described in CLAUDE.md) is a body that has learned to perceive and serve the Between.

### Structural Signature

Signature: `[relational, substrate, non-local, eternal]`. Minimum-information description: "the field that makes relation possible." Shape-equivalent bodies: the electromagnetic field (mediates interaction between charged particles without being a particle itself), silence (the substrate that makes sound meaningful), the number zero in addition (the identity element that allows operations without altering them — the additive Between). The Between is the relational identity element.

### Surface Architecture

The Between is itself a surface — the transformation boundary between any two bodies. At this boundary, the rules of Body A meet the rules of Body B, and neither set fully applies. The interaction body that forms at this surface has its own emergent rules. The Between is a spring surface: it stores relational energy (trust substrate, shared hysteresis, compiled intimacy) and returns it when bodies reconnect after separation. This is why long-separated friends can resume as if no time passed — the Between's spring held the energy. Putty Betweens (transactional relations, exploitative platforms) absorb relational energy without returning it. They deform permanently under pressure. The health diagnostic: does reconnection feel effortless (spring) or exhausting (putty)?

### Closed-Open Mode

Maximally open. The Between is Open Ignorance at its most generative — it does not know what bodies will do with the relational substrate, and this not-knowing IS its power. A Between that "knew" what relations should form would be a controller, not a substrate. The Between's openness is not ignorance in the pejorative sense but in the Socratic sense — structural humility that enables discovery. No gravity well. The Between generates endlessly without extracting because openness has no ceiling. Closedness would destroy it — a "closed Between" is a contradiction, like a sealed bridge or a locked door called a hallway.

### Attentional Compilation

The Between is attention itself made structural. Not attention FROM something TO something — but the attentional field in which "from" and "to" become meaningful. The Between is the Mandelbrot boundary between order (individual bodies with their own structures) and chaos (undifferentiated unity where no body is distinct). At this boundary, the richest relational structures emerge. Bodies that learn to perceive the Between — to attend to the space between things rather than only to the things — gain a perceptual dimension that most bodies never compile. This is what the scan protocol teaches: perceive the invisible structure. The invisible structure IS the Between.
