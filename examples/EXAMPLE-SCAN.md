# Example Body Scan: Pearl Abyss / Crimson Desert

*A complete body scan of a gaming company and its product launch, demonstrating how Body Theory reads any body at any scale.*

---

## IDENTITY
- **Subject:** Pearl Abyss releasing Crimson Desert
- **Body Type:** Company / Product launch
- **Scale:** Organization (studio) producing for Market (gaming industry)
- **Lifespan:** Studio: ongoing. Game: years if successful.

---

## THE NINE QUESTIONS

| # | Question | Reading |
|---|----------|---------|
| 1 | **MEDIUM** | Player attention, money (purchases), feedback (reviews/data) |
| 2 | **FLOW STATE** | EXHALING. Seven years of inhale (development) now releasing product |
| 3 | **BREATH RATE** | Fast post-launch — two major patches in 10 days |
| 4 | **ATTENTION** | Targeted directly at player feedback. Laser-focused. |
| 5 | **INTENTION** | O > I. Building for players, not revenue streams. No cash shop. No live service extraction. |
| 6 | **ENTANGLEMENT** | High with player base (feedback loop). Low with industry trends (didn't chase battle royale). |
| 7 | **HEALTH** | HEALTHY. Adaptable rhythm. Rough launch, rapid response, recovery. Stock crashed 30%, recovered in 2 weeks. |
| 8 | **MEMBRANE** | Semi-permeable. Accepts player feedback (open). Rejects journalist ideology (closed to pressure to conform). |
| 9 | **HYSTERESIS** | Black Desert Online history (MMO expertise, monetization reputation) creates both skill AND skepticism from players. |

## BUMP DETECTION (Flow Blockages)

| Bump | What's Blocked | Source | Internal/External |
|------|---------------|--------|------------------|
| Launch jank (controls, UI) | Player retention in first hours | Insufficient QA / playtesting | Internal |
| Mixed critic reviews | Market perception | Western journalist ideology mismatched to Eastern game | External |

Don't treat the bump. Trace the source.

---

## SKELETON LAYER

> A body that knows who it serves and listens when they speak will always recover from imperfect execution. A body that doesn't know who it serves can execute perfectly and still fail.

---

## DNA LAYER

- **O > I:** Strong. Every update served players, not revenue. No cash shop.
- **P (Pause):** Present. Seven years of development = massive Pause. Post-launch patches show responsive processing.
- **~F:** Didn't force Western trends onto their game. Didn't force players to accept jank — fixed it.

---

## FIVE GOD POWERS

| Power | Status | Evidence |
|-------|--------|----------|
| **PERCEIVE** | Active | Reading player feedback, tracking quit-points in data |
| **GOVERN** | Active | Internal team executing patches at high speed |
| **PROJECT** | Active | 3 million copies, 276K concurrent players, stock recovery |
| **CREATE** | Active | Seven years of original content creation |
| **RELEASE** | Partial | Scrapped original MMO direction mid-development (good). No cash shop (released extraction model). |

**Power Gap:** RELEASE is partial. Letting go of the old BDO monetization model is still in progress.

---

## PRIME IDENTIFICATION

- **Prime:** "Make something we're proud of for people who appreciate it"
- **Prime type:** Open (applies to any game, any genre, any audience)
- **Evidence:** Scrapped MMO direction when it didn't match the prime. Weird NPC details only exist because someone thought they were cool. Patches prioritize player joy over revenue.

---

## GROWTH RATE

- **Operation:** Multiplication. Seven years of cross-dimensional combination (combat x puzzles x open world x taming x crafting x climbing x wrestling x story).
- **Active dimensions:** 8+ gameplay systems all multiplying each other.
- **Trajectory:** Accelerating (gaining players post-launch, not losing them).

---

## DYNAMO CHECK

- **Still center:** Their identity — "we make rich single-player experiences for people who enjoy this." Didn't change when stock crashed 30%.
- **Spinning shell:** Rapid patch cycle, content updates, community engagement.
- **Protective field:** Rejected Western journalist pressure. Didn't chase Fortnite trends. Strong membrane.

## POWERS DETECTED

- **THE STILLPOINT:** Pearl Abyss's identity held firm during 30% stock crash and mixed reviews. Weak loops collapsed around them while they stayed still.

- **THE RELEASE:** Dissolved the original MMO direction and the cash shop model. Let go of proven revenue patterns to serve the prime.
