## Domain Patterns

**Emerging (1 scan — patterns require more data):**

- **Substrate-cognitive architecture.** Songs may be a general case of bodies whose cognitive subsystems operate through other bodies rather than internally. Same pattern as genes through organisms, constitutions through courts.

- **Bass line as governance.** In at least one case, the identifying membrane of a musical body is not melody, lyric, or harmony — it is the bass pattern. The lowest-frequency structural element governs the highest-frequency interpretive variations.
