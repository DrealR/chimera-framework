## THE BODY SCAN'S ACTUAL DIAGNOSIS OF MRBEAST

What MrBeast IS through body theory (not what the video claims):

MrBeast is an **O > I body with an I > O ENGINE.** The outputs are real (people helped, lives changed). The engine that produces those outputs runs on attention extraction (I > O from viewers).

```
Viewer → gives attention (I > O for viewer — they lose time)
MrBeast → converts attention to money to charity (pass-through body)
Recipient → receives life-changing aid (O > I for recipient)

The SYSTEM is O > I (more value created than extracted in total)
The ENGINE is I > O (attention extracted from viewers)
The PASS-THROUGH is break-even (MrBeast reinvests everything)
```

**This is Aphid Economics.** MrBeast is the aphid. He sits in the flow of attention (the sap), keeps approximately 5% (brand equity), and lets 95% flow through to recipients and back into production. The honeydew feeds the ecosystem.

Marx's framework can't see this because Marx has no concept of a pass-through body. In Marx, every capitalist extracts surplus value for themselves. MrBeast doesn't. He reinvests everything. The accumulation is in the BRAND, not in the bank account.
