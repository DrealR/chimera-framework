## Cross-Domain Patterns

Chess scans surface patterns that transfer to other domains:
- **Irreversibility** (pawn moves, weak structures) → economics, medicine, decisions
- **Capability vs access** (rook behind closed files) → any domain where talent exists but opportunity doesn't
- **Pathological stillness** (zugzwang, trapped pieces) → psychology, institutions, autoimmune disease
- **Minimum viable complexity** (drawn endgames) → physics, organizational theory
