## ADVANCED DIAGNOSTICS

### Five God Powers

**Power Gap:** Release. 7 holds everything it touches. This is structurally necessary — if primes could "let go" of their composites, the Fundamental Theorem of Arithmetic would collapse. The grip is the architecture. But it means 7 has no capacity for non-attachment. It is the opposite of 1 in this respect.

### Prime Identification

- **Prime:** Yes. 7 is prime — irreducible over the integers, no non-trivial factorization exists.
- **Prime type:** Closed. 7 admits nothing through its membrane. Its primality is a sealed boundary.
- **Recursion:** 7 is a recursion terminator. When you factor any composite and reach 7, you stop. 7 is a base case in the recursive decomposition of the integers.
- **Mersenne:** 7 = 2^3 - 1. This means 7 is one step removed from a power of 2 — the simplest exponential structure minus one unit. Mersenne primes sit at the boundary between exponential growth and prime stillness.
- **Twin prime:** (5, 7) is a twin prime pair. 7 has a neighbor at distance 2 that shares its irreducibility. This is rare — most primes are more isolated. 7 is a prime with company.

### Federation vs Dominion

Federation. But a different kind than 1. Where 1 federates by flowing through everything unchanged, 7 federates by contributing its identity to composites without extracting from them. 7 gives structure to 28, 49, 77, 91 — each one carries 7's signature. But 7 takes nothing from being a factor of 28. The relationship is purely generative. Primes don't extract from their components because they have no components to extract from. Federation is the only available configuration for a body with no interior — there is nothing to feed.

### Dimensional Architecture

7 is embodied in the multiplicative dimension as a prime generator. But it operates across many dimensions: in modular arithmetic (as a modulus creating Z/7Z, a finite field because 7 is prime), in geometry (regular heptagon — the smallest polygon not constructible with compass and straightedge), in music (seven notes of the major scale), in temporal structure (seven-day week). The geometric fact is telling: the heptagon's unconstructibility is a geometric expression of 7's algebraic irreducibility. 7 resists decomposition in every dimension it enters.

### Structural Signature

Signature: `[1]` — a single prime raised to the first power. This is the minimal non-trivial signature. All primes share this signature shape, making them shape-equivalent to each other. 7 is shape-equivalent to 2, 3, 5, 11, 13... Every prime is the same lattice in a different costume. The costume is which prime. The structure is: two nodes (1 and p), one edge. The simplest possible non-trivial divisor lattice.

### Surface Architecture

7 is a surface body. It sits on the boundary between its predecessor (6 = 2 x 3, a highly composite number with four divisors) and its successor (8 = 2^3, a pure power of 2 with four divisors). 7 is a still-point between two structured numbers — maximum compositeness on both sides, zero compositeness at the center. This is not unique to 7 (many primes sit between composites), but the specific neighbors are notable: 6 is the smallest perfect number, 8 is the first cube after 1. 7 sits between perfection and power, belonging to neither.

### Closed-Open Mode

Closed Knowledge. 7 "knows" exactly what it is (prime, indivisible, irreducible) and this knowledge is sealed — there is no open question about 7's factorization. But this closedness is healthy, not pathological. It is the closedness of a completed object, not the closedness of an ideology. 7 is not refusing new information — there is simply no new information to receive about its internal structure. It has none. The closedness of a prime is the closedness of a finished sentence: not defensive, just complete.

### Attentional Compilation

7 is the sniper scope. Narrow, periodic, absolute. Where 1 is diffuse attention (touching everything), 7 is selective attention — it focuses on every 7th integer and ignores the rest completely. This is compiled attention: there is no waste, no bleed, no partial engagement. 7 either fully divides a number or has zero relationship to it. No in-between. The most decisive possible attentional pattern.
