### STRUCTURAL WEAKNESS (v3.11 relational forcing)
1. **Context dependency** — Needs open space and minimal interruption. Street noise or bad surfaces can fracture the circuit.
2. **Bridge-body risk** — The three together act as a bridge between physical play and artistic expression, but we narrow it: "rhythm-to-presence transducer." No vague language.
3. **Over-stimulation gap** — The circuit can become addictive; some bodies need deliberate Release into rest.
4. **Cultural translation** — Works best in playful cultures; rigid or overly serious environments weaken it.
