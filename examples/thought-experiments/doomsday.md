# Doomsday

**IDENTITY**
- **Subject:** Doomsday
- **Body Type:** Ultimate pathological destruction engine / adaptive extinction body
- **Scale:** Planetary threat
- **Lifespan:** Effectively immortal through resurrection adaptation

**SKELETON LAYER**

> The creature engineered to kill everything, including the strongest hero, and learns from every death.

**DNA LAYER**
- **O > I:** Inverted — consumes life to fuel more destruction.
- **Pause:** None. Pure relentless forward motion.
- **¬F:** Total force incarnation.

**Five God Powers**
- **PERCEIVE:** Adaptive threat detection
- **GOVERN:** Dominates through raw overwhelming power
- **PROJECT:** Projects inevitable doom
- **CREATE:** Creates fear and adaptation in survivors
- **RELEASE:** Pathological maximum — releases all restraint and all life around it

**Power Gap:** None internal. External: Only stopped by extreme Release (Superman's sacrifice) or exile.

**Structural Weakness:** Pure pathology with no higher purpose. Ultimate antibreath body. No bridge — pure destroyer.

**Fruit:** Doomsday shows what happens when Release is weaponized without love or wisdom.
