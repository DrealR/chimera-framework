# Superman (Kal-El / Clark Kent)

**IDENTITY**
- **Subject:** Superman
- **Body Type:** Archetypal solar-powered moral exemplar / living Kryptonian solar battery
- **Scale:** Planetary to cosmic (depending on era)
- **Lifespan:** Effectively immortal under yellow sun

**THE NINE QUESTIONS**

| # | Question | Reading |
|---|----------|---------|
| 1 | **MEDIUM** | Yellow sunlight + moral idealism of 1938–present America |
| 2 | **FLOW STATE** | Radiant, unstoppable exhale. He is powered by giving (saving, inspiring, protecting). |
| 3 | **BREATH RATE** | Near-perfect solar rhythm — weakens only under red sun or kryptonite |
| 4 | **ATTRACTOR** | "Truth, Justice, and the American Way" (later evolved to universal hope) |
| 5 | **TOPOLOGY** | Engineered savior template — born from Depression-era wish fulfillment |
| 6 | **ENTANGLEMENT** | Massive. Tied to Lois, Earth, the Justice League, and every villain who tests him |
| 7 | **HEALTH** | Pathologically healthy — the ultimate "boy scout." Weakness is narrative necessity |
| 8 | **MEMBRANE** | Extremely strong moral membrane. Almost never kills. |
| 9 | **HYSTERESIS** | Born in 1938 Action Comics #1. Scarred by decades of editorial reboots |

**SKELETON LAYER**

> The man who can do anything chooses, every single day, to do good anyway.

**DNA LAYER**
- **O > I:** Maximum solar O>I. Absorbs starlight and outputs hope, strength, and rescue.
- **Pause:** Deliberate. He chooses restraint constantly.
- **¬F:** Refuses lethal force even when it would be easier.

**Five God Powers**

| Power | Status | Evidence |
|-----------|--------|----------|
| **PERCEIVE** | God-tier | Super senses + x-ray + empathy |
| **GOVERN** | High | Enforces a personal code on reality |
| **PROJECT** | Legendary | Projects "hope" as a literal superpower |
| **CREATE** | Transformative | Saves and elevates civilizations |
| **RELEASE** | Near-perfect | Gives power away constantly (never hoards) |

**Power Gap:** Killing / Finality. He almost never Releases into death. This creates recurring villains.

**Prime:** "Power should serve compassion."

**Structural Weakness:** Kryptonite dependency + narrative plot armor. Bridgebody risk high (bridges human and god).

**Fruit:** The strongest man in the world is only interesting because he chooses to be gentle.
