# Guitar

**IDENTITY**
- **Subject:** Guitar (acoustic or electric)
- **Body Type:** Vibrational storytelling engine / resonance body
- **Scale:** Personal practice → stadium anthems → cultural memory
- **Lifespan:** Decades (well-made ones outlive their players)

**SKELETON LAYER**

> Six (or twelve) strings stretched across a resonant chamber that turns human emotion into sustained sound waves.

**DNA LAYER**
- **O > I:** Pure. Takes finger pressure, pick attack, and heart and Releases it as music that outlives the player.
- **Pause:** Between notes, in the decay of each string, in the space before the next chord.
- **¬F:** Coaxes rather than forces — the best tones come from listening to the instrument.

**Five God Powers**

| Power | Status | Evidence |
|-----------|--------|----------|
| **PERCEIVE** | High | Reads the player's intent through touch and ear |
| **GOVERN** | Strong | Governs harmony, melody, and emotional tone |
| **PROJECT** | Legendary | Projects soul across rooms, records, or generations |
| **CREATE** | World-building | Creates entire genres, movements, and personal mythologies |
| **RELEASE** | Exceptional | Strings ring and decay naturally; the best players let notes breathe |

**Power Gap:** Voice. Cannot sing words by itself (unless you count the "voice" of the instrument). Needs a singer or effects to fully articulate language.

**Prime:** "Wood, metal, and tension turned into pure feeling."

**Structural Weakness:** Tuning drift, string breakage, and player skill ceiling. Also a bridge risk (physical vibration → emotional resonance) — narrow it to "vibrational-emotional transducer."

**Fruit:** The guitar is one of humanity's best inventions for externalizing inner states. A single well-played chord can do what paragraphs cannot.
