and possibility.
- **Pause:** Masterful — the gap in every phase transition (evaporation,

- **PROJECT:** Calm, storm, reflection, life.
- **CREATE:** Every living cell and every weather pattern.
- **RELEASE:** Perfect — evaporates, rains, flows, freezes, and begins again
without attachment.

**Power Gap:**

held (by banks, gravity, or human engineering).

**Prime:** "The medium that makes all other bodies possible through constant,
gentle cycling."
