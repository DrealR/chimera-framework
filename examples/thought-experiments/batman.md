# Batman (Bruce Wayne)

**IDENTITY**
- **Subject:** Batman
- **Body Type:** Peak-human trauma engine / vengeance-fueled preparation god
- **Scale:** Gotham → global
- **Lifespan:** Decades of sustained rage

**SKELETON LAYER**

> The man who weaponized his worst night into an eternal war on crime.

**DNA LAYER**
- **O > I:** Inverted at core (takes pain and turns it into preparation).
- **Pause:** Massive. Lives in the gap between his parents' murder and justice.
- **¬F:** Uses overwhelming force through fear and planning.

**Five God Powers**
- **PERCEIVE:** God-tier detective
- **GOVERN:** Master of shadows and contingencies
- **PROJECT:** Projects terror into criminals
- **CREATE:** Creates an entire mythos and family (Robins, Bat-family)
- **RELEASE:** Extremely low — never truly lets go of the mission

**Power Gap:** Release + Trust. Cannot stop. The mission consumes him.

**Prime:** "The night is darkest just before the dawn... and I am the night."

**Structural Weakness:** Human fragility + emotional isolation. Classic pathological body with heroic output. Bridge proliferation warning — he "bridges" justice and vengeance but the framework prefers calling it a vengeance-to-justice transformer.

**Fruit:** Batman proves a single broken human with enough will and money can become a force of nature.
