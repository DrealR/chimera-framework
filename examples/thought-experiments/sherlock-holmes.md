# Sherlock Holmes

**IDENTITY**
- **Subject:** Sherlock Holmes
- **Body Type:** Pure Perceive/Govern deduction engine
- **Scale:** Victorian London → global archetype
- **Lifespan:** Timeless in detective fiction

**SKELETON LAYER**

> The mind that turns observation into inevitable truth through ruthless logic and focus.

**DNA LAYER**
- **O > I:** High intellectual. Takes clues and gives solutions.
- **Pause:** Extreme — lives in the gap between evidence and conclusion.
- **¬F:** Uses mental force via deduction.

**Five God Powers**
- **PERCEIVE:** God-tier (details others miss)
- **GOVERN:** Masters crime and chaos through intellect
- **PROJECT:** Projects rational order onto mystery
- **CREATE:** Creates justice and narrative resolution
- **RELEASE:** Low — addicted to the work (cocaine as artificial release)

**Power Gap:** Emotional Release and human connection (Watson helps compensate).

**Prime:** "When you have eliminated the impossible, whatever remains, however improbable, must be the truth."

**Structural Weakness:** Social isolation and boredom pathology. Excellent diagnostic for over-Perceive bodies.

**Fruit:** The ultimate proof that pure perception, properly governed, feels like magic.
