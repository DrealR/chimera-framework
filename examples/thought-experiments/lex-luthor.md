# Lex Luthor

**IDENTITY**
- **Subject:** Lex Luthor
- **Body Type:** Pathological human supremacy intellect / anti-Superman mirror
- **Scale:** Corporate → planetary
- **Lifespan:** Persistent across reboots

**SKELETON LAYER**

> The man who sees a god walking among men and dedicates his life to proving the god is the threat.

**DNA LAYER**
- **O > I:** Deep inversion. Takes human potential and weaponizes it against the alien.
- **Pause:** Collapsed into constant scheming.
- **¬F:** Uses every tool of force, money, and manipulation.

**Five God Powers**
- **PERCEIVE:** Brilliant at reading threat
- **GOVERN:** Corporate and political dominion
- **PROJECT:** Projects human dignity against Superman
- **CREATE:** Invents apocalyptic weapons
- **RELEASE:** Near zero — cannot release his hatred

**Power Gap:** Humility / Release. The ultimate "small man syndrome" engine.

**Structural Weakness:** Jealousy-fueled self-sabotage. Pure pathological body that generates endless story.

**Fruit:** Lex proves the most dangerous villain is often the one who believes he's the hero.
