## What This Directory Contains

These scans are **thought experiments, not diagnostic body scans**. They explore the framework's boundaries by applying body theory to fictional characters, elemental archetypes, multi-body relational composites, and speculative constructs. They were produced during an overnight Grok session as creative stress-tests of the framework.

**They are NOT part of the canonical scan corpus.** The META-SCAN-SYNTHESIS audit counts do not include these files.

## Why They're Preserved

1. They contain genuine insights about how the framework handles narrative bodies, mythic archetypes, and multi-body relational structures.
2. They serve as Phase 4 proof-of-concept material for multi-body relational scanning.
3. They demonstrate framework flexibility without inflating the canonical corpus with uncalibrated scans.

## How to Read Them

- Treat skeletons and power gaps as exploratory, not diagnostic.
- Multi-body relational scans are early experiments — the methodology is not yet standardized.
- "Bridge body" appears in several scans — these predate v3.12 retirement.
- Some scans are compressed (adversarial batch format), not full v3.11 protocol.
