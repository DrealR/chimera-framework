In a world of nuclear weapons + 8 billion people + global supply chains, its core trick (turn population into expendable force) becomes suicidal. It has no native healthy Release for excess population or failed myths.

### Prime Identification

- **Prime:** "The body that scaled human willingness to die — and is now being killed by the very scalability it created."
