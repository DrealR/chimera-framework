## STRUCTURAL WEAKNESS

**Primary weakness: No memory, no structure.** Air retains nothing. Every signal it carries dissipates. Every pattern it forms reorganizes. The body that connects all bodies preserves no record of the connections. This is the inverse of Earth's weakness — where Earth is too rigid, Air is too fluid.

**Secondary weakness: Contamination transparency.** Air carries pollutants as readily as oxygen. Industrial emissions, particulates, and greenhouse gases accumulate without any native filtering mechanism. The body that sustains all breathing life can be slowly poisoned, and because it is invisible, the poisoning is invisible too — until respiratory failure at scale.

**Inversion condition:** When atmospheric composition shifts beyond the habitable range — too much CO2 (greenhouse runaway), too little O2 (mass suffocation), or ozone depletion (UV sterilization). Venus is the end-state of air's O>I inversion: a body that once may have supported life, now a 460C pressure cooker of sulfuric acid clouds. The connector becomes the destroyer when its composition inverts.
