## Pattern 3: The Membrane Taxonomy

Every physics body has a defining membrane, but the membranes differ radically in type. Three distinct categories emerged:

| Membrane Type | Bodies | Character |
|---------------|--------|-----------|
| **Absolute / non-negotiable** | Special relativity (c), black hole (event horizon) | Permanent boundary. Cannot be crossed, circumvented, or weakened. Enforced by geometry, not by force. |
| **One-way / irreversible** | Black hole (event horizon inward), quantum superposition (measurement), entropy (equilibrium boundary) | Passage is permitted in one direction only. Crossing is irreversible — putty, not spring. |
| **Invisible / pervasive** | Higgs field (vacuum itself), general relativity (curvature, no fixed boundary), quantum computing (decoherence threshold) | No sharp boundary visible. The membrane is the medium itself, or a gradual threshold rather than a wall. |

| Body | Membrane | Spring or Putty? |
|------|----------|-------------------|
| Special relativity | c (speed of light) | Spring — mass increases asymptotically as v approaches c, pushing back harder |
| General relativity | Event horizon | Spring for external observers (redshift barrier); putty for infalling observers (smooth, irreversible crossing) |
| Higgs field | Electroweak phase transition | Spring — perturbations (Higgs bosons) decay back to ground state in ~10^-22 seconds |
| Entropy | Ordered-to-disordered boundary | Putty — crossed thresholds do not spontaneously reverse |
| Quantum superposition | Measurement interaction | Putty (measurement is irreversible), but the superposition itself is spring (oscillates until disturbed) |
| Black hole | Event horizon | Putty — zero spring constant inward. The only surface in physics with no bounce-back. |
| Star | Photosphere / heliosphere | Spring — sunspots and flares are the surface springing back from magnetic compression |
| Quantum computing | Decoherence boundary | Spring via error correction (continuously pushing the system back toward coherence) |

| Quantum entanglement | No-communication theorem / decoherence boundary | Absolute correlation, no signaling — dual membrane (rigid to information, permeable to correlation) |
| Fire | Fuel/air/heat balance | Extremely sensitive — small perturbation creates massive state shift (ember → wildfire) |
| Water | Surface tension / phase boundaries | Extraordinarily permeable yet cohesive — dissolves everything, loses nothing |
| Earth | Crust / soil horizons | Thick, protective, slow — permeable to water and roots but solid to most energy |
| Air | None / mixes instantly | Most permeable membrane in physics — carries information freely, retains nothing |

**Structural law:** Physics membranes come in three structural types — walls (absolute), doors (one-way), and atmospheres (invisible/gradual). The strongest membranes in physics are not barriers but geometric properties of spacetime itself. c is not a wall; it is a shape. The event horizon is not a surface; it is a causal boundary. The most powerful boundaries are the ones you cannot see, touch, or push against — because they are built into the structure of reality, not imposed upon it.
