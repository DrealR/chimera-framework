## BUMP DETECTION

Three critical bumps. First: decoherence — the core pathology. External noise collapses the Pause prematurely, corrupting computation. This is not a bug to be fixed but a fundamental tension between computation (which requires openness to quantum effects) and isolation (which requires closure from environmental effects). The body must be simultaneously open and closed. Second: the scalability wall — current architectures face exponential overhead in error correction as qubit count grows. Third: the classical-quantum interface bottleneck — measurement destroys quantum information, and repeated measurement for error correction introduces its own noise. Every solution creates a new problem.

- **O > I or I > O:** Extreme theoretical O>I — exponential computational parallelism from linear physical resources. A 300-qubit register can represent more states simultaneously than there are atoms in the observable universe. But current practical O>I is modest: the overhead required to protect coherence (cryogenics, shielding, error correction) means the input cost is enormous relative to output. The body's theoretical O>I ratio is the highest of any engineered system. Its practical O>I ratio is currently poor. The gap between theoretical and practical IS the body's growth trajectory.
- **Pause:** Coherent evolution — the sacred interval between initialization and measurement where the qubits explore all computational paths simultaneously. This Pause is the computation. Its quality determines the output. A longer, cleaner Pause = a more powerful computer. The entire engineering effort of quantum computing is directed at extending and purifying this Pause. The Pause is not a feature of the computation — it is the computation.
- **¬F:** High in theory — the Born rule guarantees that measurement outcomes cannot be forced, and quantum algorithms work by constructive interference (amplifying correct answers) rather than by brute-force search. The algorithm does not force the answer; it makes the answer more probable. But in practice, ¬F is continuously violated by environmental noise, which forces decoherence — collapsing the Pause before the computation chooses to exhale. The body's struggle is to maintain ¬F against a universe that keeps forcing.
