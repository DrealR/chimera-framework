## SKELETON LAYER

> The villain who created your trauma will always try to reclaim the power that the trauma produced. The only way to defeat them is to GIVE the power back voluntarily, because the power was never yours to keep — it was the trauma's, and returning it dissolves both the power AND the hold.

---

## DNA LAYER

- **O > I:** Bruce's resolution is PURE O > I. "Take it. Take it all." He gives his power to the one who wants to consume it. The giving IS the victory. Taking destroyed the father. Giving freed Bruce.
- **P (Pause):** The Pause is the moment Bruce stops fighting and says "take it." The entire film builds to that one Pause.
- **~F:** Bruce tried to FORCE the Hulk away (suppression). Didn't work. The father tried to FORCE the power (absorption). Didn't work. Resolution came through ~F: surrender.
